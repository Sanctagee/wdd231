Website Project Site Plan

EdenPrep CBT Platform - Computer-Based Test Preparation for Nigerian Students

Site Name

EdenPrep CBT

This name was selected because it:

  • Incorporates "Eden" from Edenites Academy for brand recognition
  • Clearly indicates this is a preparation platform with "Prep"
  • Specifies the platform type with "CBT" (Computer-Based Test)
  • Is memorable and professional for the target audience

Optional domain availability: edenprepcbt.com or edenprepcbt.ng

Site Purpose

The EdenPrep CBT platform will provide Nigerian secondary school students with:

  • Access to practice tests for major Nigerian exams (WAEC, JAMB, NECO)
  • Performance analytics to identify strengths and weaknesses by subject area
  • Timed exam simulations to build test-taking stamina and familiarity
  • Subject-specific question banks with instant scoring and feedback
  • Progress tracking across multiple practice sessions

This platform addresses the critical need for accessible, quality test preparation resources for Nigerian students.

Scenarios

These scenarios represent questions from our target audience:

Scenario 1: JAMB Candidate

Question: "As a JAMB candidate, how can I practice with past questions under timed conditions similar to the actual exam?"

Content Response: The platform will provide timed practice sessions that mimic the actual JAMB exam format, with subject-specific question banks and a countdown timer to simulate real exam pressure.

Scenario 2: Performance Tracking

Question: "Where can I see my performance trends across different subjects to know which areas need more focus?"

Content Response: The Results Dashboard will display visual analytics showing performance by subject, topic areas, and over time, helping students identify patterns and focus their study efforts effectively.

Typography

The selected font family is Inter from Google Fonts.

  • Inter Regular (400): Body text and general content
  • Inter Medium (500): Subheadings and navigation items
  • Inter Semi-bold (600): Section headings
  • Inter Bold (700): Main headings and important callouts

Inter was selected for its excellent readability, modern appearance, and comprehensive character set support. It performs well across different screen sizes and devices.
