⭐ Cinque Stelle CBT – Site Plan

Site Name

Cinque Stelle CBT (Italian for “Five Stars CBT”) represents excellence and high achievement. It is an online learning and computer-based testing platform designed to prepare Nigerian students for national exams such as WAEC, JAMB, and NECO.

Optional Domain: cinqueacademy.com or cinquestellecbt.com

Site Purpose

The site will serve as an online hub where students can practice CBT exams, get real-time feedback, and track progress through interactive dashboards. It combines beautiful design with smart functionality to make learning enjoyable and effective.

  • Timed CBT practice tests with instant scoring
  • Personal performance analytics
  • Exam insights and question explanations
  • Information about Cinque Stelle’s academic initiatives

Scenarios

  • How can I take a JAMB or WAEC mock test online with instant grading?
  • Can I view my performance reports and track improvement by subject?
  • Where can I learn more about Cinque Stelle’s programs and mentors?

Color Schema

  • Royal Blue (#1B3C73): Navigation bar, headings, and buttons.
  • Golden Yellow (#FFD700): Stars, highlights, and action icons.
  • Deep Navy (#0A1A2F): Footer background and contrast sections.
  • Soft Gray (#F8F9FA): Background color for the site.
  • Charcoal (#333333): Primary text color.

Typography

  • Playfair Display: For titles and section headers (elegance and trust).
  • Poppins: For body text and forms (modern readability).
  • Roboto Mono: For analytics or performance data displays.
