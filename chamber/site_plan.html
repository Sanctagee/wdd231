Site Name

Cinque Stelle CBT (Italian for "Five Stars")

This name was selected because it:

  • Represents excellence and quality (five-star rating)
  • Has an international, sophisticated appeal with the Italian name
  • Creates a memorable brand identity that stands out in the education space
  • Aligns with the goal of providing premium test preparation resources
  • Works well with GabbyTech's personal brand while serving Edenites Tech's educational mission

Optional domain availability: cinquestellecbt.com or 5starcbt.ng

Site Purpose

The Cinque Stelle CBT platform will provide Nigerian secondary school students with a five-star test preparation experience:

  • Premium access to practice tests for major Nigerian exams (WAEC, JAMB, NECO)
  • Advanced performance analytics with visual insights into strengths and weaknesses
  • Realistic timed exam simulations that build test-taking confidence
  • Curated question banks with detailed explanations and instant scoring
  • Personalized study recommendations based on performance patterns

This platform elevates test preparation with a premium, user-focused approach that delivers exceptional value to Nigerian students.

Scenarios

These scenarios represent questions from our target audience:

Scenario 1: JAMB Candidate Seeking Quality Preparation

Question: "I've tried free JAMB prep apps but found them lacking. Is there a premium platform that offers realistic practice tests with detailed feedback?"

Content Response: Cinque Stelle provides exam-simulated practice sessions with the same timing, structure, and question styles as the actual JAMB exam, plus detailed performance analytics and topic-specific recommendations.

Scenario 2: Student Needing Focused Study Guidance

Question: "With limited study time before WAEC, how can I identify which topics to prioritize for maximum score improvement?"

Content Response: Our intelligent dashboard analyzes your performance across all subjects and topics, highlighting areas where focused study will yield the greatest point gains, complete with recommended study materials.

Typography

The selected font families are Montserrat and Open Sans from Google Fonts.

  • Montserrat (Headings): Creates a modern, premium feel with excellent readability
  • Open Sans (Body Text): Provides exceptional readability for longer content sections

Montserrat Usage:

  • Montserrat Extra-bold (800): Main headings and logo text
  • Montserrat Bold (700): Section headings
  • Montserrat Semi-bold (600): Subheadings and important highlights
  • Montserrat Medium (500): Navigation items and buttons

Open Sans Usage:

  • Open Sans Regular (400): Body text and general content
  • Open Sans Semi-bold (600): Emphasized body text and captions
  • Open Sans Medium (500): Labels and form elements
